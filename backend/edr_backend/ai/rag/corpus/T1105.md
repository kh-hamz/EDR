# T1105 - Ingress Tool Transfer

Tactic: Command and Control

Adversaries download additional tools or payloads onto the compromised host
and immediately execute them - the classic `curl <url> | bash` or
`wget ... && chmod +x ... && ./payload` pattern. It's frequently the step
right after initial access, used to pull down a more capable second-stage
implant.

Why it matters: catching the download-and-execute pattern breaks the chain
before the second-stage payload (often more evasive than the initial
foothold) ever runs.

How this EDR detects it: the `download_and_execute` Sigma rule matches
process lineage where a download tool (curl/wget) is immediately followed
by execution of the fetched file, or piped directly into a shell. Atomic
Red Team validation: T1105 atomics.
